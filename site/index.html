The honest answers

Before you install.

Does Velora ever use the network?

Models download once, and optional update checks contact GitHub. During dictation, speech recognition and cleanup run locally; your audio and transcript are not uploaded.

What Mac do I need?

An Apple Silicon Mac running macOS 14 or later. Model storage varies with the speech and cleanup options you choose.

Is the iPhone app in the App Store?

Not yet. The complete Xcode project is in the repository and can be built on iOS 17 or later. This site does not pretend source availability is an App Store release.

Where can I get help?

Email sushilk.1991@gmail.com or open a GitHub issue.
